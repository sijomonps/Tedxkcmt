About TEDxKCMT

TEDxKCMT, hosted annually by Kristu Jyoti College of Management and Technology, is the first institution in Kerala to host TED Circles and an exciting hub for innovation and creativity. This renowned event brings together a diverse range of speakers from across various fields to share their groundbreaking ideas and experiences. With a focus on science, technology, arts, culture, and social issues, TEDxKCMT sparks thought-provoking discussions and encourages positive change. Following the TED format, the event blends inspiring TEDTalks with live speakers, creating an immersive environment for deep discussions and connections. Attendees will also have the opportunity to engage in interactive workshops, panel discussions, and networking sessions. TEDxKCMT fosters a sense of community, encouraging collaboration and the exchange of ideas, making it an unmissable experience for all participants!
